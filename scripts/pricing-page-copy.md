## FAQ Section

**Q: How does sponsored placement work?**
Sponsored listings appear at the top of their category with a "Sponsored" label. The underlying AgentRank score isn't altered — we show your organic rank alongside your sponsored position for transparency. Developers trust the index because it's honest.

**Q: Can I sponsor a category someone else is already in?**
Only one sponsor per category for Premium Placement. Sponsored Listing allows multiple sponsors per category, but we limit it to 3. First-come, first-served.

**Q: What categories are available?**
We organize tools into ~20 categories: search/retrieval, memory/context, browser automation, data/databases, integrations, observability, documentation, frameworks, and more. We'll help you pick the right fit.

**Q: What's the traffic today?**
We're in early growth. We'll show you real traffic data before you commit — if the numbers don't justify the spend, we'll tell you. We don't want sponsors who don't get value.

**Q: Can I see analytics before deciding?**
Yes. We can give you a preview of your tool's current organic traffic on AgentRank. Reach out and we'll pull the numbers.

**Q: What if my tool isn't indexed yet?**
Not indexed = not visible. A sponsored listing gets you indexed immediately with featured placement from day one. Best time to start is before competitors claim the category.

**Q: How do I cancel?**
Email or Slack. No 30-day notice requirements. Monthly billing cancels at end of cycle.
