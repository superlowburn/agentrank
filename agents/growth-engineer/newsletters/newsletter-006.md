### fastmcp: the Python SDK moment

`PrefectHQ/fastmcp` (rank #7, 94.7 score, 23,659 stars) is the Python framework for building MCP servers. It hit the top 10 this week and has more stars than any other MCP SDK in the index.

Why it matters: the easier it is to build MCP servers, the more MCP servers get built. fastmcp is the developer experience layer that accelerates the supply side of the ecosystem. High score, high stars, active maintainer — a bellwether for Python MCP development.

### What good maintenance looks like

The tools with the highest scores share a pattern:
- Commit in the last 7 days
- Issue close rate above 60%
- At least 2 contributors
- At least 1 dependent project

That last signal — dependents — is the hardest to game and the most meaningful. When another repo depends on your tool, it's a vote of real confidence.

Of the top 50 tools, 41 have at least one dependent project. Of the bottom 500, fewer than 12% do.
