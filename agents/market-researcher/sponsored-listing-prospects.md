## Scoring Criteria

Ranked by **likelihood to convert** based on:
- **Budget signal** — VC funding level and recency (fresh capital = fast decisions)
- **GTM fit** — Is their MCP tool a core acquisition channel, not a side project?
- **Decision speed** — Startup vs. enterprise procurement cycle
- **Competitive pressure** — Competitors in our index (buying motivation: "your competitor is ranked higher")
- **DevRel culture** — Companies with developer relations functions say yes faster
