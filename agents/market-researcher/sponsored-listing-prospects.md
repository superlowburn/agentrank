## Scoring Criteria

Ranked by **likelihood to convert** on:
- **Budget signal** — funding recency and size
- **GTM fit** — MCP tool as core acquisition channel
- **Decision speed** — startup vs. enterprise procurement cycle
- **Competitive pressure** — named competitors in our index
- **DevRel culture** — companies with developer relations say yes faster
