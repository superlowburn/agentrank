## Screenshot 1 — Homepage Leaderboard
**File:** `screenshot-1-homepage.png`
**URL:** agentrank-ai.com

Capture the full homepage above the fold, showing:
- The hero headline "Find the best MCP servers and AI agent tools"
- The social proof stats bar (25,000+ tools indexed, crawled nightly, etc.)
- The install command for Claude Code: `claude mcp add agentrank -- npx -y agentrank-mcp-server`

**Goal:** Immediately communicates "this is a ranked list of real tools" and shows the install-once CTA.
